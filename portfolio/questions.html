questions clients ask

Question Answer Additional Info
What types of events do you cover? We cover a wide range of events, including weddings, corporate events, parties, and more. We specialize in capturing moments in any setting, from intimate gatherings to large corporate conferences.
What is your availability? We are available for projects year-round. Availability depends on the specific dates of your event. It’s best to book in advance, especially for busy seasons like weddings in the summer or holidays.
How long does it take to receive the final video? Typically, you will receive the final video within 4-6 weeks after the event. We aim to provide high-quality editing, so the timeline can vary based on the complexity of the project.
Do you provide drone footage? Yes, we offer drone footage for certain events, depending on the location and event requirements. Drone footage may require permits, depending on the location. Let us know in advance if you want aerial shots.
Can I request specific video styles? Absolutely! We can tailor the style of the video to your preferences, from cinematic to documentary-style editing. We can also blend styles to match your vision, so feel free to share any examples or inspirations you have!
